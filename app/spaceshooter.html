How to Play
Controls
Arrow Keys / WASD Move
Space / Enter Super Shot (when meter full)
Left Click Super Shot
ESC Pause / Resume
M Exit to Menu (from pause)
Weapons & Combinations
✦ Spread Shot — 3-way spread
⚡ Rapid Fire — Double fire rate
≡ Triple Shot — 3 parallel bullets
⟳ Bouncer Shot — Bullets bounce off walls
⤊ Piercing Rounds — Bullets hit 2 enemies
All weapons combine! Buy multiple to stack effects.
Super Shot
5 damage · Pierces everything · 60px wide beam
Fills from kills · Press Space when meter glows gold
Trail Effects
● Gold — Double Shot (double fire)
● Red — Reverse (inverted controls)
● Blue — Speed Up (boost fire + speed)
● Purple — Slow Down (slow everything)
Asteroid Belts & Shop
7 belts cycle: Normal → Speedy → Swarm → Splitter → Gravity → Mine → Ghost
Each belt ends with a boss (changes behavior at 50% and 25% HP).
Kill it → floating shop pod drifts across the screen.
Shop Pod Strategy
● Collide — Enter the shop to buy items
● Let it pass — Next shop has 3-5 items on sale (reward for patience)
● Shoot it — Earn +55pts but 2-3 items cost double on the next shop
Normally only 1 item is on sale. Plan your approach!
Category Pricing
Items grouped by category (Weapons/Defense/Utility). Buying any item doubles the price of remaining items in the same category. Choose your build wisely!
