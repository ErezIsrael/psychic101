Remote Viewing Trainer

Practice Controlled Remote Viewing (CRV) with randomized targets. Each session gives you a target code — record your impressions, then reveal the target to compare.

How It Works

  1. Get a target code — a random 8-digit number linked to a hidden image
  2. Draw your ideogram — let your hand move freely to capture the gestalt
  3. Record impressions — sensory data, dimensions, colors, emotions
  4. Reveal & compare — see the target and rate your accuracy
  5. Review & improve — track your progress over time

Quick Tips

  • Relax before starting — take 3 deep breaths
  • Iterate the target code silently: "Target ----"
  • Record everything — even if it seems silly
  • Don't force or guess — let impressions come naturally
  • Watch for AOL (analytical overlays) — label them and move on
