Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Store

Psychic101 stores data exclusively in your browser using localStorage or sessionStorage. No data is ever transmitted to a server, sent to any third party, or stored in a database.

The types of data stored include:

  • Training data: Session results, accuracy scores, ratings from the Remote Viewing Trainer, Zener Symbol Trainer, Card Guessing Trainer, Dial Trainer, and Partner Platform.
  • Journal entries: Text you write in the Psychic Journal and Dream Tracker.
  • Assessment results: Your Clair Sense profile from the Clair Sense Identifier.
  • Grounding session logs: Records of grounding exercises completed.
  • Language preference: Your chosen language (English or Hebrew).

2. No Cookies

Psychic101 does not use cookies. All persistent data is stored via localStorage or sessionStorage, which are browser-based storage mechanisms that keep data on your device only.

3. No Third-Party Services

Psychic101 does not load any external scripts, analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only external resource accessed is Wikimedia/Wikipedia images, which are fetched by the Random Object Viewer feature for training purposes.

4. Data Deletion

You can delete all stored data at any time by:

  • Clearing your browser's site data for this domain
  • Using your browser's "Clear browsing data" feature
  • Deleting individual entries within the app

Instructions vary by browser. In most browsers, go to Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data, and select "Cookies and other site data" or "Local storage data".

5. Legal Basis

This policy complies with the Israeli Privacy Protection Law (PPL), Amendment 13. Since data is stored only on your device and never transmitted, the risk to your privacy is minimal. However, we believe in full transparency about what data is stored and why.

6. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or data requests, please contact us via the project repository at GitHub.
