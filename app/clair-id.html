The 8 Clair Senses

  • 👁 Clairvoyance — Clear seeing, visual impressions
  • 👂 Clairaudience — Clear hearing, auditory impressions
  • ✋ Clairsentience — Clear feeling, tactile/emotional sensations
  • 💡 Claircognizance — Clear knowing, information without source
  • 👃 Clairalience — Clear smelling, scent impressions
  • 👅 Clairgustance — Clear tasting, flavor impressions
  • 💜 Clairempathy — Clear empathy, absorbing others' emotions
  • 🌐 Clairambience — Clear ambience, sensing energy of places
